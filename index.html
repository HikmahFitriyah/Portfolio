Nur Hikmah Fitriyah

Using art to explore, question, and communicate scientific and health experiences
Researcher Artist Science Communicator

Hi, I am Hikmah!

My work investigates how visual art can function as a tool for communication, reflection, and inquiry in health and scientific contexts. Situated at the intersection of art, health, and education, my projects explore how creative practices can make complex ideas more accessible while creating space for reflection on patient experience and medical knowledge.

I currently work as a research coordinator in oncology research, supporting multidisciplinary research projects while developing experience in research design, data collection, analysis, and academic publication.

Research Interests

  • Art for scientific communication

  • Art for conveying lived experiences

  • Art as inquiry and knowledge-making
